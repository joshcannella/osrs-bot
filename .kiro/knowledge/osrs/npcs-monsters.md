# OSRS NPCs and Monsters

Source: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/

## NPC Overview

NPCs (Non-Player Characters) include:
- **Monsters** - Attackable creatures
- **Friendly NPCs** - Quest givers, shop keepers, bankers
- **Slayer monsters** - Require Slayer level to damage
- **Bosses** - High-level monsters with unique mechanics

## Common Training Monsters

### Low Level (1-30 Combat)

**Chickens** (Level 1)
- **Location**: Lumbridge (north and south)
- **HP**: 3
- **Drops**: Feathers (5-15), bones, raw chicken
- **Notes**: No food required, good for absolute beginners

**Cows** (Level 2)
- **Location**: Lumbridge (north of castle), Al Kharid
- **HP**: 8
- **Drops**: Cowhide, bones, raw beef
- **Notes**: Cowhides can be tanned for profit

**Goblins** (Level 2-5)
- **Location**: Lumbridge, Goblin Village
- **HP**: 5-12
- **Drops**: Goblin mail, bones
- **Notes**: Aggressive, good for early combat

**Frogs** (Level 3)
- **Location**: Lumbridge Swamp
- **HP**: 5
- **Drops**: Bones, frog spawn
- **Notes**: Very weak, near fishing spots

### Mid Level (30-70 Combat)

**Hill Giants** (Level 28)
- **Location**: Edgeville Dungeon
- **HP**: 35
- **Drops**: Big bones (329 gp), limpwurt root, giant key
- **Notes**: Safespots available, good for F2P

**Moss Giants** (Level 42)
- **Location**: Catacombs of Kourend, Varrock Sewers
- **HP**: 60
- **Drops**: Mossy key, big bones
- **Notes**: Higher HP than Hill Giants

**Fire Giants** (Level 86)
- **Location**: Catacombs of Kourend, Waterfall Dungeon
- **HP**: 111
- **Drops**: Rune scimitar, fire runes
- **Notes**: Good XP, safespots available

**Chaos Druids** (Level 13)
- **Location**: Edgeville Dungeon (Wilderness), Chaos Druid Tower
- **HP**: 20
- **Drops**: Grimy herbs (ranarr, harralander), runes
- **Notes**: Very profitable for low level, aggressive

### Crabs (AFK Training)

**Sand Crabs** (Level 15)
- **Location**: Hosidius beach, Crabclaw Isle, Avium Savannah
- **HP**: 60
- **Drops**: None
- **Notes**: Low defence, auto-aggro, resets after 10 minutes

**Rock Crabs** (Level 13)
- **Location**: North of Rellekka
- **HP**: 50
- **Drops**: None
- **Notes**: Similar to sand crabs, less crowded

**Ammonite Crabs** (Level 25)
- **Location**: Fossil Island
- **HP**: 100
- **Drops**: None
- **Requirements**: Bone Voyage quest
- **Notes**: Best crab for training, highest HP

**Gemstone Crab** (Level 160)
- **Location**: Tlati Rainforest (moves every 10 minutes)
- **HP**: Infinite
- **Drops**: Gems (if top 16 damage dealers)
- **Requirements**: Children of the Sun quest
- **Notes**: Best for high-level training, infinite HP

## Slayer Monsters

### Popular Slayer Monsters

**Aberrant Spectres** (Level 96, 60 Slayer)
- **Location**: Slayer Tower, Catacombs of Kourend
- **HP**: 90
- **Drops**: Herbs, mystic staves
- **Notes**: Requires nose peg/slayer helmet

**Abyssal Demons** (Level 124, 85 Slayer)
- **Location**: Slayer Tower, Catacombs of Kourend
- **HP**: 150
- **Drops**: Abyssal whip (1/512), abyssal dagger
- **Notes**: Very profitable, barrage in Catacombs

**Bloodvelds** (Level 76-81, 50 Slayer)
- **Location**: Slayer Tower, Catacombs of Kourend
- **HP**: 120-130
- **Drops**: Blood runes, rune items
- **Notes**: Good XP, barrage in Catacombs

**Dust Devils** (Level 93, 65 Slayer)
- **Location**: Smoke Dungeon, Catacombs of Kourend
- **HP**: 105
- **Drops**: Dragon chainbody (rare), dust battlestaff
- **Requirements**: Face mask/slayer helmet
- **Notes**: Excellent barrage task

**Gargoyles** (Level 111, 75 Slayer)
- **Location**: Slayer Tower
- **HP**: 105
- **Drops**: Granite maul, rune items, alchables
- **Requirements**: Rock hammer to finish
- **Notes**: Very profitable, 500K-1M GP/hour

**Kurasks** (Level 106, 70 Slayer)
- **Location**: Fremennik Slayer Dungeon
- **HP**: 97
- **Drops**: Leaf-bladed battleaxe, herbs
- **Requirements**: Leaf-bladed weapons or magic
- **Notes**: Profitable, 500K-800K GP/hour

**Nechryaels** (Level 115, 80 Slayer)
- **Location**: Slayer Tower, Catacombs of Kourend
- **HP**: 105
- **Drops**: Rune items, death runes
- **Notes**: Excellent barrage task, spawns death spawns

**Smoke Devils** (Level 160, 93 Slayer)
- **Location**: Smoke Devil Dungeon
- **HP**: 185
- **Drops**: Occult necklace (1/350 from boss)
- **Notes**: Best barrage task, very fast XP

### Slayer Bosses

**Alchemical Hydra** (Level 426, 95 Slayer)
- **Location**: Karuulm Slayer Dungeon
- **HP**: 1,100
- **Drops**: Dragon hunter lance, hydra leather
- **Notes**: 3-4M GP/hour, requires hydra task

**Cerberus** (Level 318, 91 Slayer)
- **Location**: Taverley Dungeon
- **HP**: 600
- **Drops**: Primordial, pegasian, eternal crystals
- **Notes**: 2-3M GP/hour, requires hellhound task

**Kraken** (Level 291, 87 Slayer)
- **Location**: Kraken Cove
- **HP**: 255
- **Drops**: Trident of the seas, Kraken tentacle
- **Notes**: Very AFK, requires cave kraken task

**Thermonuclear Smoke Devil** (Level 301, 93 Slayer)
- **Location**: Smoke Devil Dungeon
- **HP**: 240
- **Drops**: Occult necklace, smoke battlestaff
- **Notes**: Requires smoke devil task

## Boss Monsters

### Beginner Bosses

**Giant Mole** (Level 230)
- **Location**: Falador Park
- **HP**: 200
- **Drops**: Mole skin, mole claw (for bird nests)
- **Notes**: Burrows underground, easy mechanics

**Barrows Brothers** (Levels 98-115)
- **Location**: Barrows
- **HP**: 100-120 each
- **Drops**: Barrows equipment (1/17 per piece)
- **Notes**: 6 brothers, requires 43 Prayer

**Sarachnis** (Level 318)
- **Location**: Forthos Dungeon
- **HP**: 400
- **Drops**: Cudgel, sarachnis cudgel
- **Notes**: Simple mechanics, good for learning

**Obor** (Level 106, F2P)
- **Location**: Edgeville Dungeon
- **HP**: 168
- **Drops**: Hill giant club, rune items
- **Requirements**: Giant key (from Hill Giants)

**Bryophyta** (Level 128, F2P)
- **Location**: Varrock Sewers
- **HP**: 128
- **Drops**: Bryophyta's staff, nature runes
- **Requirements**: Mossy key (from Moss Giants)

### Intermediate Bosses

**Vorkath** (Level 732)
- **Location**: Ungael
- **HP**: 750
- **Drops**: Skeletal visage, dragonbone necklace, superior dragon bones
- **Requirements**: Dragon Slayer II
- **Notes**: 2-4M GP/hour, consistent profit

**Zulrah** (Level 725)
- **Location**: Zul-Andra
- **HP**: 500
- **Drops**: Toxic blowpipe, magic fang, serpentine visage
- **Requirements**: Regicide quest
- **Notes**: 2-3M GP/hour, rotation memorization

**Dagannoth Kings** (Levels 303)
- **Location**: Waterbirth Island
- **HP**: 255 each
- **Drops**: Dragon axes, berserker/warrior/seers rings
- **Notes**: 3 kings (Rex, Prime, Supreme), tribrid setup

**God Wars Dungeon Bosses** (Levels 596-650)
- **Commander Zilyana** (Saradomin): Armadyl crossbow, saradomin sword
- **General Graardor** (Bandos): Bandos chestplate/tassets
- **Kree'arra** (Armadyl): Armadyl chainskirt/helmet
- **K'ril Tsutsaroth** (Zamorak): Zamorakian spear, staff of the dead
- **Notes**: 70+ combat stats recommended, 40 KC to enter boss room

### Advanced Bosses

**Chambers of Xeric (CoX)** (Raids 1)
- **Location**: Mount Quidamortem
- **Drops**: Twisted bow, ancestral robes, dragon claws
- **Notes**: 3-5M GP/hour in teams, scalable difficulty

**Theatre of Blood (ToB)** (Raids 2)
- **Location**: Ver Sinhaza
- **Drops**: Scythe of vitur, avernic defender hilt, justiciar armour
- **Notes**: 5-8M GP/hour, difficult mechanics

**Tombs of Amascut (ToA)** (Raids 3)
- **Location**: Jaltevas Pyramid
- **Drops**: Tumeken's shadow, Masori armour, Elidinis' ward
- **Requirements**: Beneath Cursed Sands quest
- **Notes**: 4-6M GP/hour, invocation system

**Nex** (Level 1001)
- **Location**: God Wars Dungeon (Ancient Prison)
- **HP**: 3,200
- **Drops**: Torva armour, Zaryte crossbow
- **Notes**: 3-5M GP/hour, team content

**The Nightmare** (Level 814)
- **Location**: Sisterhood Sanctuary
- **HP**: 2,400-4,800 (scales with players)
- **Drops**: Inquisitor's armour, nightmare staff
- **Notes**: Group boss, long kills

**Phosani's Nightmare** (Level 1160)
- **Location**: Sisterhood Sanctuary
- **HP**: 4,000
- **Drops**: Same as Nightmare, better rates
- **Notes**: Solo version, very difficult

## NPC Mechanics

### Aggression
- **Aggressive NPCs**: Auto-attack players within range
- **Tolerance**: Aggression resets after ~10 minutes (varies)
- **Combat level**: Some NPCs only aggro players below certain combat level

### Safespots
- **Definition**: Locations where melee NPCs can't reach player
- **Common**: Behind rocks, trees, fences
- **Usage**: Allows safe ranging/maging of dangerous monsters

### Multi-Combat
- **Single-combat**: Only one NPC can attack player at a time
- **Multi-combat**: Multiple NPCs can attack simultaneously
- **Indicator**: Crossed swords icon on minimap

### Respawn Times
- **Standard**: 25-50 seconds
- **Bosses**: Varies (some instant, some minutes)
- **Slayer bosses**: Usually instant or very fast

## Monster Weaknesses

### Combat Triangle
- **Melee monsters**: Weak to magic
- **Ranged monsters**: Weak to melee
- **Magic monsters**: Weak to ranged

### Specific Weaknesses
- **Dragons**: Dragonbane weapons (dragon hunter lance/crossbow)
- **Demons**: Demonbane weapons (Arclight, silverlight)
- **Undead**: Salve amulet, crumble undead spell
- **Kalphites**: Keris weapons
- **Vampyres**: Blisterwood weapons, Ivandis flail
