# OSRS Current Meta and Strategies (2026)

Last updated: March 2026

## Meta Overview

The "meta" refers to the most efficient, popular, or optimal strategies currently used by the OSRS community. Metas change with:
- Game updates and balancing
- New content releases
- Community discoveries
- Price fluctuations

## Combat Meta

### Melee Meta
**Best General Setup (2026):**
- **Weapon**: Scythe of vitur (multi-target) / Fang (single-target, high defence)
- **Armour**: Torva (general) / Inquisitor's (crush) / Bandos (budget)
- **Spec weapon**: Dragon claws / Dragon warhammer / Bandos godsword

**Training Meta:**
- **1-30**: Waterfall Quest
- **30-50**: Crabs (AFK)
- **50-99**: Nightmare Zone (Dharok's at 92+ HP, Obsidian before)
- **Alternative**: Slayer (profit + XP)

**DPS Priority:**
1. Strength to 99 (highest max hit)
2. Attack to 99 (accuracy for higher tier weapons)
3. Defence to 99 (last priority for pure DPS)

### Ranged Meta
**Best General Setup (2026):**
- **Weapon**: Twisted bow (high magic targets) / Bow of faerdhinen (general) / Zaryte crossbow (ruby bolt spec)
- **Armour**: Masori (f) > Armadyl > Black d'hide
- **Spec weapon**: Zaryte crossbow (ruby bolts)

**Training Meta:**
- **1-50**: Quests, crabs
- **50-75**: Cannon + slayer
- **75-99**: Chinning maniacal monkeys (expensive, fast) OR slayer (profit)

**Blowpipe Nerf:**
- Blowpipe nerfed in 2021, still good but no longer dominant
- Bow of faerdhinen and Twisted bow are now preferred for most content

### Magic Meta
**Best General Setup (2026):**
- **Weapon**: Tumeken's shadow (best overall) / Sanguinesti staff / Harmonised nightmare staff
- **Armour**: Ancestral > Virtus > Ahrim's
- **Spec weapon**: Volatile nightmare staff

**Training Meta:**
- **1-55**: Quests, Fire Strike
- **55-99**: Slayer with barrage (profit + XP) OR High Alch (passive)
- **Fast XP**: Maniacal monkeys with barrage

**Spellbook Meta:**
- **Standard**: High alch, teleports, utility
- **Ancient**: Barrage for multi-combat (slayer, PvP)
- **Lunar**: Vengeance, spell book swap
- **Arceuus**: Thralls (extra DPS), reanimation

## Slayer Meta

### Optimal Slayer Master Rotation
- **Turael/Spria**: Skip bad tasks (resets streak)
- **Duradel**: Main master (best task list, 15 points/task)
- **Konar**: Every 10th task for point boost (18 points, 90 bonus)

### Block List (Duradel)
Block tasks with high weight and low XP/profit:
1. Hellhounds
2. Greater demons
3. Black demons (unless doing demonic gorillas)
4. Fire giants
5. Abyssal demons (if not profitable at your level)
6. Spiritual creatures

**Note**: Block list varies by preference (XP vs profit vs enjoyment)

### Best Slayer Tasks (XP)
1. **Smoke devils** - Barrage in Catacombs
2. **Nechryaels** - Barrage in Catacombs
3. **Dust devils** - Barrage in Catacombs
4. **Abyssal demons** - Barrage in Catacombs
5. **Dagannoth** - Cannon + melee
6. **Kalphites** - Cannon in Kalphite Lair

### Best Slayer Tasks (Profit)
1. **Hydra** - 3-4M GP/hour (95 Slayer)
2. **Cerberus** - 2-3M GP/hour (91 Slayer)
3. **Demonic gorillas** - 1-2M GP/hour (Monkey Madness II)
4. **Gargoyles** - 500K-1M GP/hour (75 Slayer)
5. **Kurasks** - 500K-800K GP/hour (70 Slayer)

### Slayer Gear Meta
- **Slayer helmet (i)**: Always use on task (15% damage/accuracy boost)
- **Cannon**: Use whenever possible for multi-target DPS
- **Barrage**: Ancient Magicks for multi-combat tasks
- **Expeditious bracelet**: Speed up bad tasks
- **Bracelet of slaughter**: Extend good tasks

## Meta Philosophy

### Efficiency vs Enjoyment
- **Efficient**: Maximize XP/GP per hour
- **Enjoyable**: Play content you find fun
- **Balance**: Most sustainable long-term

### "Sweaty" vs Casual
- **Sweaty**: Tick manipulation, optimal methods, no breaks
- **Casual**: AFK methods, slower but relaxed
- **Both valid**: Choose based on goals and lifestyle

### Meta Changes
- Stay updated via wiki, Reddit, content creators
- Test new methods before committing
- Don't be afraid to deviate from meta for fun
