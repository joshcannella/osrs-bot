# OSRS Locations and Coordinates

Source: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/

## Coordinate System

OSRS uses a tile-based coordinate system:
- **Format**: (x, y, plane)
- **Plane**: 0 = ground level, 1 = first floor, 2 = second floor, 3 = third floor
- **Regions**: World divided into 64x64 tile regions
- **Chunks**: Regions divided into 8x8 tile chunks

## Major Cities

### Lumbridge
- **Location**: Central Misthalin
- **Coordinates**: ~3222, 3218, 0 (castle)
- **Features**: 
  - Starting location for new players
  - Bank (top floor of castle)
  - General store, furnace, spinning wheel
  - Combat tutors (free starter items)
  - Lumbridge Swamp (south) - fishing, mining
- **Teleports**: Home teleport (30 min cooldown), Lumbridge teleport (31 Magic)

### Varrock
- **Location**: North of Lumbridge
- **Coordinates**: ~3213, 3424, 0 (center)
- **Features**:
  - Grand Exchange (northwest)
  - East and West banks
  - Rune shop (Aubury's), staff shop (Zaff's)
  - Varrock Sewers (moss giants, zombies)
- **Teleports**: Varrock teleport (25 Magic), Chronicle (south of city)

### Falador
- **Location**: West of Lumbridge/Varrock
- **Coordinates**: ~2965, 3379, 0 (center)
- **Features**:
  - West and East banks
  - Party Room
  - Crafting Guild (south)
  - Motherlode Mine (Dwarven Mine)
- **Teleports**: Falador teleport (37 Magic)

### Ardougne
- **Location**: West Kandarin
- **Coordinates**: ~2662, 3305, 0 (market)
- **Features**:
  - North and South banks
  - Market (silk, fur, spices)
  - Zoo (south)
  - Legends' Guild (east, requires quest)
- **Teleports**: Ardougne teleport (51 Magic), Ardougne cloak (diary reward)

### Edgeville
- **Location**: North of Varrock
- **Coordinates**: ~3087, 3496, 0
- **Features**:
  - Bank (closest to Wilderness)
  - Edgeville Dungeon (Hill Giants, Chaos druids, Wilderness access)
  - Amulet of glory teleport destination
- **Teleports**: Amulet of glory

### Camelot (Seers' Village)
- **Location**: Central Kandarin
- **Coordinates**: ~2757, 3477, 0
- **Features**:
  - Bank
  - Camelot Castle
  - Seers' Village (flax field, agility course)
- **Teleports**: Camelot teleport (45 Magic)

### Yanille
- **Location**: Southwest Kandarin
- **Coordinates**: ~2606, 3093, 0
- **Features**:
  - Bank
  - Nightmare Zone (north)
  - Watchtower (teleport with quest)
  - Magic Guild
- **Teleports**: Watchtower teleport (58 Magic, requires quest)

### Prifddinas (Elf City)
- **Location**: Tirannwn
- **Coordinates**: ~3263, 6065, 0 (center)
- **Features**:
  - Best skilling hub in game
  - Multiple banks
  - Zalcano, Gauntlet
  - Crystal singing, divine potions
- **Requirements**: Song of the Elves quest
- **Teleports**: Crystal teleport seed, Eternal teleport crystal

## Wilderness Levels

Wilderness level = combat level range for PvP:
- **Level 1-10**: Low risk, some resources
- **Level 10-20**: Chaos altar (prayer training), Chaos Elemental
- **Level 20-30**: Green dragons, Lava maze
- **Level 30-40**: Mage Arena, Chaos Fanatic
- **Level 40-50**: Wilderness Agility Course, Scorpia
- **Level 50+**: Deep Wilderness, highest risk

**Safe Zones**: Ferox Enclave (free-for-all area with bank)

## Coordinate Lookup

For scripting, coordinates can be found:
- **RuneLite**: Developer tools → Tile location plugin
- **OSRS Wiki**: Many pages list coordinates
- **In-game**: Right-click "Examine" on tiles with developer mode

## Region IDs

Regions are 64x64 tile areas:
- **Region ID formula**: `((x >> 6) << 8) | (y >> 6)`
- **Used for**: Instance detection, area-specific scripts
- **Example**: Lumbridge castle region = 12850
