# Huntsman's Kit

Members-only storage item from Hunters' Rumours (Varlamore). Holds up to 32 different item types. Items can't be used while stored. Kept on death. Only one kit per player.

## Obtaining

Dropped from hunters' loot sacks (adept/expert/master) at 1/50 rate. Can be traded to Guild Scribe Verity for a master-level loot sack (excludes uniques).

## Storable Items

**Tools:** Bird snare, Noose wand, Butterfly net, Magic butterfly net, Butterfly jar, Box trap, Rabbit snare, Unlit torch, Small fishing net, Rope, Magic box, Imp-in-a-box, Teasing stick, Hunter's spear

**Camouflage Gear:** Wood camo top/legs, Polar camo top/legs, Jungle camo top/legs, Desert camo top/legs

**Hunter Armour:** Larupia hat/top/legs, Graahk headdress/top/legs, Kyatt hat/top/legs

**Guild Hunter Outfit:** Headwear, top, legs, boots

**Skillcape:** Hunter hood, Hunter cape, Hunter cape(t)

**Other:** Ring of pursuit, Impling jar, Horn of plenty, Horn of plenty (empty), Gryphon feather

## Notable Unstorable Items

Knife, Ferret, Spotted cape, Jar generator, Chisel, Trapper's tipple, Hunter/Super hunter potion, Hunting mix, Clockwork, All bird houses, Strung rabbit foot, Quetzal whistles, King worm, Coins, Hammer, Imcando hammer, Hunter kit, Lit torch, Tinderbox, Meat/fur pouches, Magic secateurs, Chinchompas (grey/red/black — removed March 2024), Anti-odour salt

## Tips

- Stores imp-in-a-box, making it useful as an unlimited deposit box during extended combat trips
- Empties from the last slot first
- Cannot store itself (easter egg message)
