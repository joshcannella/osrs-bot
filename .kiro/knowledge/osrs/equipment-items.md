# OSRS Equipment and Items

Source: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Armour/Highest_bonuses

## Best in Slot (BiS) Overview

BiS equipment varies by:
- Combat style (melee/ranged/magic)
- Attack type (stab/slash/crush for melee)
- Activity (general combat, bossing, PvP)
- Budget

**Important**: Use DPS calculator to compare setups: https://tools.runescape.wiki/osrs-dps/

Don't judge weapons by max hit alone - weapon speed matters!

## Melee BiS (High-End)

### Slash Setup
- **Head**: Oathplate helm (+10 slash)
- **Neck**: Amulet of rancour (+25 slash, requires 90 HP)
- **Body**: Oathplate chest (+16 slash)
- **Legs**: Oathplate legs (+12 slash)
- **Feet**: Avernic treads (max) (+5 slash)
- **Cape**: Infernal cape (+4 slash, requires Inferno completion)
- **Hands**: Ferocious gloves (+16 slash, requires 80 Att/Def)
- **Ring**: Bellator ring (+20 slash, requires Desert Treasure II)
- **Weapon**: Soulreaper axe (+134 slash, 2h) OR Zombie axe (+105 slash, 1h)
- **Shield**: Avernic defender (+29 slash, if using 1h weapon)

### Stab Setup
- **Weapon**: Osmumten's fang (+105 stab) OR Dual macuahuitl (+115 stab, 2h)
- **Ring**: Treasonous ring (i) (+8 stab)
- Rest similar to slash setup with stab-focused pieces

### Crush Setup
- **Head**: Inquisitor's great helm (+8 crush)
- **Body**: Inquisitor's hauberk (+12 crush)
- **Legs**: Inquisitor's plateskirt (+12 crush)
- **Weapon**: Inquisitor's mace (+95 crush, 1h) OR Elder maul (+135 crush, 2h)
- **Ring**: Tyrannical ring (i) (+8 crush)

### Strength Bonus (Max Hit)
Key items for maximum melee damage:
- **Amulet of torture** - +10 strength bonus
- **Fire cape / Infernal cape** - +4 / +8 strength
- **Ferocious gloves** - +14 strength
- **Primordial boots** - +5 strength
- **Berserker ring (i)** - +8 strength

## Progression Milestones

### Early Game (F2P)
- **Melee**: Rune scimitar (40 Attack), Rune platebody, Rune platelegs
- **Ranged**: Maple shortbow, Coif, Leather body
- **Magic**: Staff of air, Blue wizard robes

### Mid Game (Members, 60-70 stats)
- **Melee**: Dragon scimitar, Dragon defender, Fighter torso, Dragon legs
- **Ranged**: Rune crossbow, Black d'hide, Ava's accumulator
- **Magic**: Iban's staff (u), Mystic robes, God cape

### High Game (80+ stats)
- **Melee**: Abyssal whip, Barrows gloves, Fire cape, Bandos armour
- **Ranged**: Blowpipe, Armadyl armour, Rigour prayer
- **Magic**: Trident of the swamp, Ahrim's, Occult necklace, Augury prayer

### End Game (90+ stats, high wealth)
- **Melee**: Scythe of vitur, Inquisitor's, Avernic defender, Infernal cape
- **Ranged**: Twisted bow, Masori (f), Zaryte vambraces
- **Magic**: Tumeken's shadow, Ancestral, Tormented bracelet

## Important Utility Items

### Teleportation
- **Ring of dueling** - Duel Arena, Castle Wars, Ferox Enclave
- **Games necklace** - Barbarian Outpost, Corporeal Beast, Wintertodt
- **Amulet of glory** - Edgeville, Karamja, Draynor, Al Kharid
- **Ring of wealth** - Grand Exchange, Falador, Miscellania
- **Construction cape** - Unlimited house teleports
- **Quest cape** - Legends' Guild teleport
- **Achievement diary rewards** - Various teleports

### Inventory Management
- **Rune pouch** - Holds 3 types of runes (16,000 each)
- **Herb sack** - Holds 30 of each grimy herb
- **Gem bag** - Holds 60 of each uncut gem
- **Looting bag** - Doubles inventory in Wilderness
- **Seed box** - Holds 6 types of seeds (2^31-1 each)

### Quality of Life
- **Graceful outfit** - Reduces weight, restores run energy faster
- **Slayer helmet (i)** - 15% damage/accuracy boost on task
- **Ava's device** - Auto-picks up ammo
- **Ring of life** - Auto-teleports at low HP
- **Ring of recoil** - Reflects damage back to attacker

## Special Attack Weapons

### Common Special Attacks
- **Dragon dagger** (25% energy): 2 hits, increased accuracy/damage
- **Dragon claws** (50% energy): 4 hits, special damage distribution
- **Armadyl godsword** (50% energy): High damage, restores prayer
- **Saradomin godsword** (50% energy): Heals HP equal to 50% damage, restores prayer 25%
- **Bandos godsword** (50% energy): Drains Defence, restores HP
- **Dragon warhammer** (50% energy): Reduces target Defence by 30%
- **Toxic blowpipe** (50% energy): Doubles accuracy, heals 50% damage
- **Crystal halberd** (30% energy): Hits 2 targets, increased damage

## Degradable Equipment

Some items degrade with use:
- **Barrows equipment** - 15 hours of combat, repairable
- **Trident of the seas/swamp** - Charged with coins/scales
- **Blowpipe** - Charged with scales and darts
- **Scythe of vitur** - Charged with blood runes and vials
- **Tumeken's shadow** - Charged with chaos/death/soul runes

## Untradeable BiS Items

Must be earned, cannot be bought:
- **Fire cape** - Fight Caves (TzHaar)
- **Infernal cape** - Inferno (hardest PvM challenge)
- **Barrows gloves** - Recipe for Disaster quest
- **Fighter torso** - Barbarian Assault minigame
- **Void knight equipment** - Pest Control minigame
- **Slayer helmet (i)** - Slayer + NMZ points
- **Dragon defender** - Warriors' Guild
- **Avernic defender** - Dragon defender + Avernic hilt (Theatre of Blood)
