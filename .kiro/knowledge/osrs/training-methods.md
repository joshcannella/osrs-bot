## Melee Training Quick Reference

### Quest Start (Levels 1-45 Attack)
Complete these quests for instant melee levels:
- **Waterfall Quest** - 13,750 Attack + 13,750 Strength XP (gets you to 30/30 from level 1)
- **Fight Arena** - 12,175 Attack XP
- **Tree Gnome Village** - 11,450 Attack XP
- **The Grand Tree** - 18,400 Attack XP (requires 25 Agility)
- **Holy Grail** - 15,300 Defence XP (requires 20 Attack, Merlin's Crystal)
- **Scorpion Catcher** - 6,625 Strength XP
- **Horror from the Deep** - 4,662.5 Strength XP

Total: 65,275 Attack, 27,537.5 Strength, 15,300 Defence → Level 45 Attack, 37 Strength, 31 Defence

### Levels 30-50: Crabs
- **Ammonite Crabs** (Fossil Island) - 100 HP, low defence, auto-aggro, requires Bone Voyage
- **Sand Crabs** (Hosidius/Crabclaw Isle) - 60 HP, low defence, auto-aggro
- **Gemstone Crab** (Tlati Rainforest) - Infinite HP, best for medium/high combat, requires Children of the Sun

Crabs reset aggro after ~10 minutes; walk away to reset.

### Levels 50-70: Nightmare Zone (Normal)
- Location: North of Yanille
- Requires: 5+ quests that NMZ uses
- Use absorption + overload potions (bought with NMZ points)
- Most AFK training method in game
- Safe deaths, instanced
- Earn points for imbues and herb boxes

### Levels 70-99: Nightmare Zone (Hard)
- **Dharok's setup** - Optimal at 92+ Hitpoints (set effect scales with low HP)
- **Obsidian setup** - Obsidian armour + Berserker necklace + Toktz-xil-ak (optimal below 92 HP)
- Fastest solo melee XP/hour
- Alternative: Slayer for profit + combat XP

## Slayer Training Quick Reference

Source: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Slayer_training

### Slayer Masters

| Master | Location | Combat Req | Points/Task | Best For |
|--------|----------|------------|-------------|----------|
| Turael/Aya | Burthorpe | Any | 0 | Task skipping |
| Mazchna/Achtryn | Canifis | 20 | 6 | Low level |
| Vannaka | Edgeville Dungeon | 40 | 8 | Mid level |
| Chaeldar | Zanaris | 70 | 10 | Mid-high level |
| Konar quo Maten | Mount Karuulm | 75 | 18 | Location-specific tasks |
| Nieve/Steve | Tree Gnome Stronghold | 85 | 12 | High level |
| Duradel/Kuradal | Shilo Village | 100 combat + 50 Slayer | 15 | Best XP/hour |
| Krystilia | Edgeville | Any (Wilderness) | 25 | Wilderness tasks |

### Slayer Strategy
- Use highest level master available (Duradel for max efficiency)
- Block/skip undesirable tasks using points or Turael
- Train in multicombat areas (Catacombs of Kourend)
- Use barrage spells, chinchompas, or dwarf cannon for multi-target damage
- Slayer helmet (i) provides massive damage boost on task
- Slayer is profitable and gives passive combat XP

### Quest Requirements for Slayer Tasks
Key quests that unlock slayer monsters:
- **Priest in Peril** - Unlocks Morytania (aberrant spectres, abyssal demons, bloodvelds, etc.)
- **Dragon Slayer I** - Unlocks all dragon tasks
- **Lost City** - Unlocks mutated zygomites
- **Horror from the Deep** - Unlocks dagannoth
- **Monkey Madness II** - Unlocks demonic gorillas (black demon alternative)
- **Desert Treasure I** - Unlocks dust devils
- **Bone Voyage** - Unlocks Fossil Island wyverns

## Efficiency Principles

### Opportunity Cost
When choosing training methods, consider:
- Time spent training vs time spent making money
- Example: 2 hours killing hobgoblins for profit < 1 hour making money + 1 hour training crabs

### Balanced Training
- Maintain balanced melee stats until reaching BiS weapon/armour requirements
- Then prioritize: Strength → Attack → Defence for fastest max melee
- Exception: Specific gear requirements (e.g., 60 Attack for dragon weapons)

### AFK vs Active
- **AFK methods**: Crabs, NMZ with absorption potions, ammonite crabs
- **Active methods**: Slayer, bossing, Wilderness training
- Choose based on attention availability

### Temporary Boosts
Skills can be boosted above base level:
- Potions (super attack, super strength, ranging potion, etc.)
- Prayers (Piety, Rigour, Augury)
- Equipment bonuses
- Food (pies, stews - spicy stew gives ±5)
- Forestry worlds (+1 Woodcutting per nearby player, up to +10)

Boosts allow access to content above base level temporarily.
