# OSRS Money Making Guide

Source: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Money_making_guide

## Overview

Money making methods vary greatly in GP/hour based on:
- Skill levels
- Combat stats
- Quest completion
- Gear owned
- Grand Exchange prices (fluctuate constantly)

**Important**: Always test methods with small sample size before investing heavily, as GE prices may differ from listed values.

## Money Making Categories

### Combat
- Bossing (high level, high profit)
- Slayer tasks (consistent profit + XP)
- Wilderness activities (high risk, high reward)
- Barrows, God Wars Dungeon, Chambers of Xeric, Theatre of Blood, etc.

### Skilling
- Runecraft (high profit, slow)
- Farming (passive income via herb runs)
- Hunter (chinchompas, implings)
- Smithing (blast furnace, cannonballs)
- Cooking (high volume, low margin)

### Merchanting
- Grand Exchange flipping (buy low, sell high)
- High risk, high reward
- Requires capital and market knowledge
- Look for unstable but frequently traded items

### Recurring Methods
Passive income that doesn't require constant attention:
- **Herb runs** - Plant herbs, return later to harvest (ranarr, snapdragon, torstol)
- **Birdhouse runs** - Hunter XP + nest loot every 50 minutes
- **Farming contracts** - Complete for seed packs
- **Battlestaves** - Daily purchase from Zaff (Varrock medium diary+)
- **Miscellania** - Kingdom management for passive resources
- **Herb boxes** - Purchase from NMZ with points

## Early Game Money Making (Low Requirements)

### Stronghold of Security
- Location: Barbarian Village
- Reward: 10,000 GP + fancy/fighting boots
- Requirements: ~15 Defence recommended, authenticator required
- One-time reward

### Collecting Items
- **Cowhides** - Kill cows, tan hides, sell leather
- **Feathers** - Kill chickens, collect feathers
- **Bones** - Big bones from Hill Giants (Edgeville Dungeon)
- **Zamorak wines** - Telegrab in Wilderness (33 Magic)

### F2P Methods
- **Spinning flax** - Buy flax, spin into bowstring
- **Making gold amulets** - Craft and sell
- **Smelting iron ore** - Blast Furnace or regular furnace
- **Cutting yew logs** - 60 Woodcutting, slow but consistent

## Mid-Level Money Making

### Slayer (50-70 Combat)
- Consistent profit from drops
- Gargoyles, Kurasks, Nechryaels are profitable
- Use Slayer helmet (i) for damage boost

### Barrows (70+ Combat)
- Requirements: 43 Prayer (protect prayers), decent combat stats
- Profit from barrows equipment
- ~500K-1M GP/hour depending on luck and speed

### Blast Furnace (60+ Smithing)
- Smelt bars efficiently
- Runite bars most profitable (85 Smithing)
- Steel bars good mid-level option

### Farming (32+ Farming)
- **Ranarr weed** - Consistent profit, ~5 patches
- **Snapdragon** - Higher profit, higher level
- **Herb runs** take ~10 minutes, can do multiple per day

### Hunter (63+ Hunter)
- **Red chinchompas** - High profit, competitive
- **Black chinchompas** - Higher profit, Wilderness (PvP risk)

## High-Level Money Making (80+ Stats)

### Bossing
- **Vorkath** - 2-4M GP/hour (Dragon Slayer II required)
- **Zulrah** - 2-3M GP/hour (Regicide required)
- **Chambers of Xeric (CoX)** - 3-5M GP/hour in teams
- **Theatre of Blood (ToB)** - 5-8M GP/hour in experienced teams
- **Nex** - 3-5M GP/hour
- **Phosani's Nightmare** - High profit, very difficult

### Runecraft (77+ Runecraft)
- **Blood runes** - 500K-700K GP/hour, very AFK
- **Wrath runes** - 1M+ GP/hour, less AFK
- **Lava runes** - Fast XP, moderate profit

### Smithing (88+ Smithing)
- **Rune platebodies** - Consistent profit at Blast Furnace

### Thieving (90+ Thieving)
- **Pickpocketing elves** - 2-3M GP/hour
- **Pickpocketing vyres** - 1-2M GP/hour + Prayer XP

## Untradeable Supplies

Some methods use "zero-time" untradeable supplies gathered passively:
- **Crystal shards** - From Prifddinas activities
- **Marks of grace** - From Agility courses
- **Slayer points** - From Slayer tasks
- **Nightmare Zone points** - From NMZ

These can be converted to profit via:
- Divine potions (crystal shards + regular potions)
- Amylase crystals (marks of grace)
- Herb boxes (NMZ points)

## Grand Exchange Tips

### Buy Limits
Items have 4-hour buy limits (varies by item):
- Common items: 10,000-25,000
- Rare items: 10-100
- Check wiki for specific limits

### GE Tax
- 1% tax on most items (max 5M per transaction)
- Applies to seller, not buyer
- Reduces inflation

### Price Checking
- **Guide price** - May lag behind actual market price
- **OSBuddy/RuneLite** - Real-time price data
- **OSRS Wiki prices** - https://prices.runescape.wiki/

### Flipping Strategy
1. Find items with high trade volume and price volatility
2. Place buy offers below market price
3. Place sell offers above market price
4. Profit from spread (minus 1% tax)
5. Requires patience and capital

## Opportunity Cost

When choosing money making methods:
- **Time vs Profit**: Is it worth spending 2 hours for 200K when you could make 1M/hour elsewhere?
- **Enjoyment factor**: Burning out on optimal methods reduces overall efficiency
- **Skill training**: Sometimes training a skill to unlock better methods is more efficient long-term

## Ironman Money Making

Ironmen can't trade, so money making focuses on:
- **Alching** - High alch valuable drops
- **Shops** - Sell items to shops
- **Agility Pyramid** - 10K per lap (30 Agility)
- **Slayer** - Primary income source
- **Bossing** - Alch valuable uniques

## Efficiency Tips

1. **Use best gear available** - Faster kills = more GP/hour
2. **Optimize inventory** - Minimize banking trips
3. **Use teleports** - Save time traveling
4. **Track profits** - Use RuneLite plugins to monitor GP/hour
5. **Stay updated** - Prices change, methods become obsolete
6. **Consider supplies** - Factor in potion/food costs

## Warning: Scams

Common scams to avoid:
- **Doubling money** - No one doubles money for free
- **Phishing links** - Never enter login info on external sites
- **Lures** - Don't follow strangers to Wilderness
- **Trust trades** - Never drop items or trade in stages
- **Fake giveaways** - Streamers don't ask for items first
