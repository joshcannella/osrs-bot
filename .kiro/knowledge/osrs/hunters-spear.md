# Hunter's Spear

Thrown ranged weapon from Varlamore. Requires 55 Ranged and 30 Strength to equip.

## Creation

- **Skill:** 60 Fletching (not boostable)
- **Tool:** Knife
- **Materials:** 1 Teak logs + 5 Jerboa tails + 5 Hunter spear tips → 5 Hunter's spears
- **XP:** 9.5 Fletching XP per set
- **Ticks:** 3 (1.8s) per action

## Combat Stats

- Ranged attack: +73
- Melee strength: +48 (unique — uses melee str for ranged damage)
- Prayer: +1
- Attack speed: 5 ticks rapid, 6 ticks accurate/longrange
- Range: 5 tiles (7 on longrange)
- One-handed, weapon slot
- Ava's devices work with it

## Special Properties

- Acts as a teasing stick when equipped, with +5% success rate on pitfall creatures (Kyatt, Graahk, Larupia)
- Must be equipped to tease (can't tease from distance despite being thrown)
- Not consumed when attacking chinchompas to reset spawns (as of Feb 2026)
- Storable in the Huntsman's kit
- Tradeable on GE (~195 gp, daily volume ~6,720)
