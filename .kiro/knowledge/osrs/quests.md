# OSRS Quests

Source: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Quests

## Quest Overview

- **Total quests**: 160+ (constantly updated)
- **Total quest points**: 300+ available
- **F2P quests**: 22 quests, 44 quest points
- **Members quests**: 140+ quests, 250+ quest points

## Quest Benefits

1. **XP rewards** - Skip early level grinds
2. **Item unlocks** - Access to unique equipment
3. **Area unlocks** - New locations and content
4. **Transportation** - Teleports, shortcuts, fairy rings
5. **Money making** - Recipe for Disaster unlocks Culinaromancer's chest
6. **Slayer tasks** - Many monsters require quest completion

## Optimal Quest Order

See: https://oldschool.runescape.wiki/w/Optimal_quest_guide

The optimal quest guide orders quests to:
- Minimize skill training between quests
- Use quest XP rewards efficiently
- Unlock important content early
- Complete prerequisite chains efficiently

## Essential Early Quests

### Immediate Priority (No Requirements)
- **X Marks the Spot** - 300 coins, 200 Mining XP, 200 Smithing XP, tutorial for clue scrolls
- **Cook's Assistant** - 300 Cooking XP, access to Lumbridge range
- **Sheep Shearer** - 150 Crafting XP, 60 coins
- **Romeo & Juliet** - 5 quest points (for GE unlock)
- **Waterfall Quest** - 13,750 Attack XP, 13,750 Strength XP (level 1 → 30)

### Important Early Members Quests
- **Druidic Ritual** - Unlocks Herblore skill
- **Rune Mysteries** - Unlocks Runecraft skill (F2P too)
- **Lost City** - Unlocks dragon weapons, fairy rings (with other quests)
- **Priest in Peril** - Unlocks Morytania region
- **Fairytale I & II** - Unlocks fairy ring network
- **Animal Magnetism** - Unlocks Ava's device (ranged)
- **Monkey Madness I** - Dragon scimitar, training caves

## Major Quest Lines

### Recipe for Disaster (RFD)
- **Subquests**: 10 subquests, each rescues a council member
- **Reward**: Barrows gloves (best-in-slot gloves for most content)
- **Requirements**: Many other quests, various skill levels
- **Final fight**: Culinaromancer (175 quest points required)

**Barrows Gloves Progression:**
- Bronze gloves → Iron → Steel → Black → Mithril → Adamant → Rune → Dragon → Barrows

### Dragon Slayer Series
- **Dragon Slayer I** - Unlocks dragon equipment, green d'hide body (F2P available)
- **Dragon Slayer II** - Unlocks Vorkath boss, Myth's Guild, assembler upgrade

### Monkey Madness Series
- **Monkey Madness I** - Dragon scimitar, Ape Atoll training
- **Monkey Madness II** - Demonic gorillas, heavy ballista, Kruk

### Fremennik Series
- **The Fremennik Trials** - Access to Fremennik equipment
- **The Fremennik Isles** - Helm of neitiznot, Jatizso/Neitiznot
- **The Fremennik Exiles** - Basilisk jaw, V's shield

### Desert Treasure Series
- **Desert Treasure I** - Ancient Magicks spellbook, ice gloves
- **Desert Treasure II** - Awakened bosses, Virtus equipment, combat achievements

### Elf Series
- **Regicide** - Access to Tirannwn, Zulrah
- **Mourning's End Part I & II** - Death altar, dark beasts
- **Song of the Elves** - Prifddinas city, Zalcano, Gauntlet, crystal equipment

- **100 QP** - Antique lamp (2,500 XP)

- **250 QP** - Antique lamp (15,000 XP)

- **Requirements**: All quests completed
- **Perks**: 
  - Unlimited teleport to Legends' Guild
  - +9 prayer bonus
  - Trimmed version available with all Achievement Diaries

## Quest Difficulty Tiers

### Novice
- Short quests, minimal requirements
- Examples: Cook's Assistant, Sheep Shearer, Romeo & Juliet

### Intermediate
- Moderate length, some skill/quest requirements
- Examples: Lost City, Waterfall Quest, Tree Gnome Village

### Experienced
- Longer quests, multiple requirements
- Examples: Monkey Madness I, Legends' Quest, Lunar Diplomacy

### Master
- Long, complex quests with high requirements
- Examples: Recipe for Disaster, Desert Treasure I, Mourning's End Part II

### Grandmaster
- Longest, most difficult quests
- Examples: Dragon Slayer II, Monkey Madness II, Song of the Elves, Desert Treasure II
