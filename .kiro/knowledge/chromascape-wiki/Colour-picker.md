### Step 1: Taking a Screenshot

To take a screenshot:

1. Open the ChromaScape web UI.
2. Open your OSRS client.
3. Activate the Screenshotter button.

Once it completes, a temporary screenshot will be saved to the `output/` directory as `original.png`.  
You **do not** need to modify this file or do anything with it.

---

### Step 2: Using the Sliders

In the web UI:

1. Click the **Colour Picker** button in the top-right corner.
2. Push the `min` HSV sliders up until your colour almost disappears.
3. Push the `max` HSV sliders down until your colour is isolated
3. Give your colour a name and submit it.

Once submitted, your named colour is now usable.

**You may also choose to use the colour directly in code as a variable**
In that case, copy the code output and reference it directly in code.

Refer to the [Making Your First Script](./Making-Your-First-Script) wiki page for an example of how to use it in your automation code.
